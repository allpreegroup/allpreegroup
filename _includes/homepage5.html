HOW IT WORKS

      1: Reach out to our HR department on WhatsApp, and they will request the necessary details to set up your account within our organization manually.

      2: Purchase our Physical Reusable QR-Scan Gift Card, enabling you to shop in-store at over 300+ partner locations islandwide.

      3: Purchase the amount of Shopping eVoucher in advance based on your spending pattern for the month,

      4: Visit one of our supported merchants and shop as usual. At checkout, swipe the merchant's rewards card if you have one. Indicate that you'll pay with GiftMe, then show the QR Code (on the back of your Reusable Gift Card) to the cashier for scanning. Complete the transaction by entering your PIN.
